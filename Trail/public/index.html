About Us!

Trail is a web app built for people who love hiking but don’t love spending ages searching for the right route. It helps you quickly find trails near you that match your experience level, preferred distance, and difficulty. Trail provides clear ratings, estimated completion times, elevation, and easy-to-understand descriptions. You can save favorites, submit condition reports, and plan safely. Offline support, light/dark mode, and a clean interface make Trail simple and helpful for every hiker.

Why Choose Trail?

Trail is designed to make hiking simple, safe, and enjoyable. We provide clear difficulty ratings, estimated times, and real-time trail conditions. Save your favorite trails, plan adventures offline, and get personalized recommendations. Trail helps you make informed choices so every hike is an unforgettable experience, whether you’re a beginner or an expert hiker.
